# Pricing Intelligence Platform

## Overview

The Pricing Intelligence Platform is an AI-powered SaaS application designed to help SaaS founders and product managers optimize their pricing strategy. The platform automatically tracks competitor pricing, analyzes billing data from Stripe/Paddle integrations, and provides actionable AI-powered recommendations to maximize revenue. It features real-time competitor monitoring, industry benchmarks, pricing analytics dashboards, and A/B test calculators to help companies make data-driven pricing decisions.
